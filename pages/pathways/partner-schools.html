Where you study shapes how you stay

We've partnered with some of Canada's most respected Designated Learning Institutions — colleges, polytechnics, and universities that combine world-class teaching with practical, hands-on learning, affordable tuition, and strong support for international students once they arrive.

Every school on this page is PGWP-eligible, meaning their graduates can apply for a Post-Graduation Work Permit and begin building the Canadian work experience that opens the door to permanent residence.
